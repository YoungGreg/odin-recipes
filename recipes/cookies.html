Cookies

Description

Make these cookies for the holidays. They add great variety and originality to your cookie trays.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup shortening
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose-flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 (9 ounce) package condensed mincemeat, crumbled

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375F (190 deg C)
  2. Cream together shortening and sugar until fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating after each addition. Sift together the flour, baking and salt; stir into the creamed mixture. Finally, stir in the mincemeat.
  3. Drop dough by teaspoons onto the prepared baking sheets. Bake 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven. Remove the baking pans to cool on wire racks.
