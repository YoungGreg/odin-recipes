Snickerdoodle

Description:

The history of this whimsically named treat is widely disputed, but the popularity of this classic cinnamon-sugar-coated cookie is undeniable! â€”Taste of Home Test Kitchen

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup butter,softened
  • 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons sugar, divided
  • 1 large egg, room temperature
  • 1/2 teaspoon vinilla extract
  • 1-1/2 cups all-purpose-flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375. Cream butter and 1 cup sugar until light and fluffy; beat in egg and vinilla. IN another bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda and cream of tartar; gradually beat into creamed mixture.
  2. In a small bowl, mix cinnamon and remaining sugar. Shape dough into 1-in balls; roll in cinnamon sugar. Place 2 in, apart on ungreased baking sheets
  3. Bake until light brown, 10-12 minutes. Remove from pans to wire racks to cool
